# Botter

**Configure a bot. Deploy it into the dungeon. Watch it loot.**

Botter is *Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup* reimagined as an idle game.
Instead of playing a hero, you outfit an autonomous bot, send it
into procgen dungeons across 20+ branches, and watch it fight,
die, and gear up over hundreds of runs.

The dungeon, monsters, items, and vault library are ported in
spirit from DCSS — two decades of polished, playtested content.
The bot AI, idle reward loop, and progression curve are original.

## What's in the build

- 5 tiers of branches: Dungeon → Lair/Forest/Orc/Temple →
  Snake/Spider/Swamp/Shoals/Hive → Vaults/Crypt/Tomb/Elf/Depths →
  Forge/Glacier/Slime/Labyrinth/Abyss/Pandemonium/Zot
- 1300+ DCSS vaults procgen-placed across floors
- Loot with rarity tiers, affixes, legendary uniques
- Spell archetypes: multicast, forked, frostbite, comet trail, etc.
- Bot upgrades, stat allocation, branch modifiers
- Outpost between runs for browsing, equipping, choosing branches

## Status

Early friends-only playtest. Things are rough, balance is wobbly,
the web build takes 15-25s to first-load (browser texture upload
cost). Once loaded, runs play at 120fps. Steam + mobile are the
real targets; itch is for early feedback.
