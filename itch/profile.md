# deanyo

Building **[Botter](https://deanyo-gh.itch.io/botter-idle)** — DCSS reimagined as an idle game.

Configure a bot, deploy it into procgen dungeons, watch it fight and
loot across 20+ branches. The dungeon, monsters, items, and vault
library are ported in spirit from Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup; the bot
AI and idle loop are original.

Currently in early playtest. Steam + mobile are the eventual targets.
