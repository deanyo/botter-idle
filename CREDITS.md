# Credits

## Botter

Designed and built by **Dean Ryan**.

AI-assisted iteration with Claude Code (Anthropic).

## Inspirations

- **Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup** — for two decades of dungeon-generation,
  monster, and item design that this project leans on heavily for its
  shape, vault library, and visual identity. https://crawl.develz.org/
- **Melvor Idle**, **RuneScape**, **Path of Exile**, **WoW dungeon
  systems** — for the loops and mechanics layered on top of the
  DCSS frame.

## Bundled third-party content

- **DCSS / RLTiles tile sprites (CC0)** — the 32×32 tileset that gives
  Botter its visual identity. Contributors are listed in `NOTICE.md`.
  Sourced from the public DCSS tile pack at
  https://github.com/crawl/tiles/.
- **DCSS vault layouts (GPLv2+ source, ported to JSON shape data)** —
  1320 vault layouts authored by DCSS .des contributors, listed by
  handle in `NOTICE.md`.
- **Godot Engine (MIT)** — https://godotengine.org/
- **GUT — Godot Unit Test (MIT)** — https://github.com/bitwes/Gut
